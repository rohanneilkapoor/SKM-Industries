Capabilities and Equipment

SKM Industries is your source for quality CNC machining for metal parts, plastics, and high precision assemblies. We give you the benefit of over 30 years of industry experience and the latest in equipment and computer technology.

CAD/CAM System - Virtual Gibbs (Solid Surfacer)

Mill, Advanced Mill, and 3-D Surfacer capable of 3-D rendering of the part being cut for verification of the part program, programming from DXF, IGES, SOLID WORKS, PARA SOLIDS & SAT files or direct input from blue prints. Design changes or modifications of the part or programs can be incorporated on the fly

3 MAZAK Turning Centers

NEXUS QT 100N (6"chuck, 6000 rpm, 10 hp): equipped with a bar feeder, tool touch probe, and automatic tool life management

2 MAZAK Turning Centers

Smart QT 100N (6"chuck, 6000 rpm, 10 hp): tool touch probe and automatic tool life management

3 HAAS Milling Centers

VF 3SS (40"X20"X24" in XYZ travels, 10000 rpm, 10 hp): equipped with automatic pallet changers, high speed machining, and a side mount tool changer, spindle and tool touch probes

4 HAAS Milling Centers

DT-1 (24" X 16" X 16" in XYZ travels, 15000 rpm, 10 hp): equipped with automatic pallet changers, high speed machining, and side mount tool changers, spindle and tool touch probes

3 HAAS Milling Centers

VF - 2SS (30"x16"x24" in XYZ travels, 10000 rpm, 10 hp): equipped with a full fourth axis, spindle, and tool touch probes

Quality Assurance System

Complies with MIL-I-45208, Micro Vue Excel 502 HC Fully Automated Production CMM with optical and probe capabilities, in addition to height gauges, sets of pin gauges, surface plates, calipers, micrometers, thread gauges, bore gauges and indicators (on regular calibration schedules)

The Latest in CNC Machining Technology

Technology is making rapid advancements in the manufacturing industry. Our goal at SKM Industries is to remain at the forefront of these changes, giving you the advantage of high efficiency and precision, resulting in overall cost effectiveness and unsurpassed quality.

1. Our CNC Machining Centers are the very latest in machining technology.
2. All equipment is networked to a central CAD/CAM system for efficient programming.
3. Programming can be done using DXF and IGES files or blueprints and verified using 3-D rendering before machining.
4. Continuous maintenance support from our CAD/CAM manufacturers provides automatic and software upgrades.

Integrated Information Management

SKM Industries' Custom Information System is designed to manage and integrate data for each customer work order. All customer data, such as quality assurance specifications, part materials, or processes is organized and stored in the system, easily accessible upon request or for repeat orders.

Quality Control Management

SKM is committed to quality and excellence for every customer we serve, from independent consultants to major corporations. Our focus on quality is an integral part of our daily operation. We set the highest standards for ourselves, enabling us to meet and exceed your exacting specifications.

We implement Inspection travelers at our production stations to identify inspection and dimension criteria for the machine operators and quality assurance personnel. We can meet or exceed MIL-I-45208 system compliance.

Since our establishment in 1992, SKM Industries has built a strong industry reputation for providing highly accurate, quality parts with a high level of customer service and attention.
